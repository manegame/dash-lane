INtrodUCTION

Technology has been around for ages. Digital technology for a bit less long. I have the feeling that, since technology is always changing, we shouldn’t treat it as a full grown entity. Technology has a far-reaching influence.

I plead for an approach that treats technology and how it relates to its users as pubescent. The state in which the technology is finding out its own place in the world, where it’s exploring its body and where it’s growing hard. It’s insecure and awkward but it has the energy to rebel.

I will use methods, aesthetics, associations, ways of thinking, tutorials, guide books, news outlets, hobbies, environments, personages, interfaces, ideas and infrastructures of the tech-for-capital domain and use these to crack them up and open. It’s key to show what happens inside the belly of the beast.

I wanted to graduate but then I also want to be connected the whole time and just chill.... There is so much to see and to discuss and stillllllllllll stuckkkkkkkkkk sometimes sssssss. How when where who, it's all things I care for and care about. But the truth is, what about compulsive behaviours? What about the control we don't always have to stop, or say no. Where does that get a place? Lost control.

Dread and stuckness and repetition and oversaturation are the motivators. For change.....///??????
